What is CircleBind?

It is the procedure we take to protect you.

Tamperproof
All your belongs are locked and tied inside a protective sheet. The only way to enter into your belongings is by damaging the sheet.
Legally binding
Contracts will be signed by all three parties. Any breaches of said contract, for example damage to protective sheet, will be dealt with appropriately.
Assessment
Students wanting to rent out their room must pass an eligibility assessment. This will involve a face-to-face meet between the two students.
Neat
By keeping the stored items hidden under a sheet, your room remains tidy and the storage less intrusive.
Communication
Students will be given each others contact information to streamline communication and improve confidence.
Dirt-free
The protective sheet ensures that your room stays clean from any dirt that may come from the other student's storage.
